Gigantes

Gigantes is a popular Greek dish

made with giant butter beans. It's typically made by simmering the beans in a tomato-based sauce

with garlic, onions, and herbs like oregano and thyme. The beans are typically

served with rice, crusty bread, or a simple Greek salad on the side.

The dish is hearty, healthy, and comforting.

ingredients

  • 2 cups dried giant butter beans, soaked overnight
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh oregano, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons fresh thyme, chopped
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps :

  1. In a large pot, saute the onion and garlic in olive oil until softened.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, water, wine, tomato paste, and herbs.
  3. Stir in the soaked beans and simmer for 1-2 hours, or until the beans are tender and the sauce is thickened.
