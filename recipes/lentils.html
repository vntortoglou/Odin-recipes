Lentils Soup

Lentil soup is simple, yet nutritious dish

It is made with lentils, vegetables, and broth.

It has a hearty texture and a slightly earthy flavor, making it perfect for a cozy and comforting meal.

It can be served as is, or with a dollop of yogurt or sour cream, or topped with chopped parsley or croutons.

ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps :

  1. In a large pot, saute the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic in a bit of oil or butter until softened.
  2. Add the lentils, broth, bay leaf, thyme, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  4. Remove from heat and let cool slightly. Serve with a dollop of yogurt or sour cream, and enjoy!
