Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish

Made of alternating layers of wide, flat pasta, tomato sauce, cheese, and other ingredients.

The pasta is often boiled until al dente, then placed in a baking dish and layered with

ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and the tomato sauce.

The layers are baked together until the cheese is melted and the sauce is bubbling.

It's often served with garlic bread and a salad on the side.

ingredients :

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesa cheese
  • Garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper (optional, to taste)

Steps :

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the meat (if using), tomato sauce, and any additional vegetables or ingredients, if using.
  3. Assemble the lasagna in a baking dish by layering the pasta, sauce, cheeses, and any other ingredients in the order of your choice.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees F for about 30-40 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
